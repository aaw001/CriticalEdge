Pharmacology in EMS — Getting It Right

Choose meds for effectiveness, stability, and simplicity under pressure.

Why EMS meds differ

  • Environment: heat/cold, vibration
  • Timing: interventions must matter now
  • Simplicity: packaging, dosing, training

Selection criteria

  • Evidence of outcome benefit (not just surrogate endpoints)
  • Stability & shelf‑life in real field conditions
  • Clear dosing that reduces error under stress

Checklist (downloadable pack coming)

  • Benefit proven in similar setting?
  • Stable & simple to deploy?
  • Standardised concentrations & labels?
  • Usage being tracked vs outcomes?
