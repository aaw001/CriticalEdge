Stroke Recognition in the Field

FAST exam, check glucose, confirm last‑known‑well, and pre‑alert a stroke centre.

Why this matters

Time is brain; field recognition unlocks thrombolysis/thrombectomy pathways.

Assessment: FAST

  • Face
  • Arm
  • Speech
  • Time (LKW)

Prehospital actions

  • ABCs, oxygen only if hypoxic
  • Check/correct glucose
  • Pre‑alert and transport fast
