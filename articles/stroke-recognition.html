Stroke Recognition in the Field

At‑a‑glance: FAST exam, check glucose, confirm last‑known‑well, and pre‑alert a stroke center.

Why this matters

Stroke is time‑critical…

Assessment: FAST

  • Face droop
  • Arm weakness
  • Speech difficulty
  • Time — last‑known‑well

Actions (prehospital)

  • ABCs; oxygen if hypoxic
  • Check glucose
  • Pre‑alert stroke center
