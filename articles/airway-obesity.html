Airway in the Obese Patient

Ramped position, suction ready, backup airway, induction=LBW; NMB=TBW.

Why this matters

Obesity reduces FRC and safe apnea time; first‑pass success is critical.

Setup

  • Ramped, head‑elevated
  • Pre‑oxygenate (consider CPAP)
  • Backup SGA

Dosing

  • Induction by lean body weight
  • Neuromuscular blockers by total body weight
